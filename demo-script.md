# Chandlr Demo Script — 60 seconds

## Before recording

- Open Excel with a PO sheet (use the Tesco test PO or a real one)
- Have ERP price data in a second sheet
- Sideload the add-in so Copilot agent is available
- Set screen resolution to 1920x1080, zoom Excel to 120%
- Close all other tabs/notifications

---

## Script

### 0:00–0:05 | Hook
**Screen:** Excel open with a messy PO spreadsheet visible
**Say:** "This is a real purchase order. Forty lines, three price columns, inconsistent SKU formats. Sound familiar?"

### 0:05–0:15 | Extract
**Click:** Open Copilot side panel, type: *"Extract the order data from this PO"*
**Screen:** Watch the staging sheet appear with clean, standardized columns
**Say:** "One prompt. Chandlr reads the PO, detects the columns, validates the data, and gives you a clean staging sheet."

### 0:15–0:30 | Reconcile
**Click:** Switch to ERP sheet, select the price data range
**Click:** Back to Copilot, type: *"Reconcile the PO against the ERP data I've selected"*
**Screen:** Reconciliation results sheet appears — green/yellow/red rows
**Say:** "Now it compares every line against your ERP prices. Green is a match. Yellow is within tolerance. Red needs attention. Instantly."

### 0:30–0:40 | Resolve
**Click:** Type: *"Generate a credit note and create an ERP staging sheet"*
**Screen:** Credit note sheet + ERP staging sheet created
**Say:** "Credit note generated. ERP staging sheet ready — color-coded so you know exactly which lines to enter and which to hold."

### 0:40–0:50 | Intelligence
**Click:** Type: *"Show me price trends from my reconciliation history"*
**Screen:** Dashboard sheet appears with metrics, top movers, watch list
**Say:** "Every reconciliation builds your price history. Chandlr spots trends, flags anomalies, and tells you which SKUs to watch."

### 0:50–0:60 | Close
**Screen:** Cut to landing page or logo
**Say:** "Chandlr. Order processing with an intelligence layer. Works inside Excel, powered by Copilot. Try it free — link in the description."

---

## Tips

- Don't rush the reconciliation reveal (0:25) — the color-coded rows are the money shot
- If Copilot takes a beat to respond, that's fine — it looks real and builds anticipation
- Keep your voice steady and matter-of-fact — operators trust calm competence, not hype
- If you flub a line, just pause and redo that section — you can cut in post

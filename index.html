The moat

An intelligence layer on top of order processing

Chandlr is both an Excel add-in and a Microsoft 365 Copilot agent. Click buttons or speak plain English.

Copilot agent

Registered as a declarative agent in Microsoft 365. Process orders by talking to Copilot.

"Extract the PO data from this sheet"
"Reconcile against the ERP prices"
"Generate a credit note"
"Show me price trends"

Smart extraction

Upload CSV, Excel, or PDF. Chandlr auto-detects columns — SKU, price, qty, description, UOM — from any format. No templates. No mapping rules.

Price intelligence

Every reconciliation builds a price history. Chandlr detects trends, flags anomalies, and surfaces the SKUs drifting toward exceptions — before they hit your margin.

Why Chandlr

Built for order processing teams

Works with any ERP

SAP, Oracle, D365, NetSuite. Chandlr stages data in Excel — you enter it however you normally do. No API connectors.

Prefix SKU matching

PO says "1046", ERP says "1046V001"? Matched automatically. Case-insensitive, whitespace-tolerant.

Color-coded everything

Green = match. Yellow = check. Red = stop. Every output sheet is instantly readable.

Full audit trail

Results, credit notes, re-invoices, ERP staging, and price history — all saved as sheets in the workbook.

Data quality gate

9 validation rules catch duplicates, missing prices, quantity outliers, and line total mismatches before reconciliation.

Your data stays local

Everything runs inside your Excel workbook. No data is sent to external servers. Nothing leaves the spreadsheet.
